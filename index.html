What we do?

We started our journey in Oct, 2019. We try to engage students developers through projects, events & meetups.

When you join binary, you'll have the opportunity to meet local developers with similar interests in technology. A binary meetup event includes talks on a wide range of technical topics where you can learn new skills through hands on workshops. The binary community prides itself on being an inclusive environment for everyone and anyone from beginner developers to experienced professionals - all are welcomed to join
